FIRST AID

PURPOSE

TO PROVIDE FIRST AID AWARENESS AND BASIC LIFE SUPPORT TRAINING TO THE COMMUNITY. This program aims to equip individuals with the knowledge and skills necessary to respond effectively in emergency situations, potentially saving lives and reducing the severity of injuries.

CONTENT

  • WOUNDS AND BLEEDING
    • BURNS AND FRACTURES
    • EMERGENCY SCENE MANAGEMENT
    • CARDIO-PULMONARY RESUSCUTATION (CPR)
    • RESPIRATORY DISTRESS EG: CHOCKING,BLOCKED AIRWAY
